Q9.Compare the results of Univariate and Bivariate analysis for UCI diabetes dataset
Aim:

To compare univariate and bivariate analysis on a diabetes dataset.

Algorithm:

Import Pandas, Matplotlib, and Seaborn.

Load diabetes dataset.

Plot univariate (histogram) and bivariate (scatter/correlation) graphs.

Program:
import pandas as pd
import seaborn as sns
import matplotlib.pyplot as plt

df = pd.read_csv("diabetes.csv")

# Univariate
df['Glucose'].hist()
plt.title('Univariate Analysis - Glucose')
plt.show()

# Bivariate
sns.scatterplot(x='Glucose', y='Insulin', data=df)
plt.title('Bivariate Analysis - Glucose vs Insulin')
plt.show()

Output:

Univariate: Histogram of Glucose

Bivariate: Scatterplot of Glucose vs Insulin

Result:

Univariate shows single variable distribution, while bivariate shows relationships between two variables.
